# LocalBiz Connect 2.0

**Tagline:** Empowering Local Businesses with a Social, Interactive, and Growth-Focused Platform.

---

## **Project Overview**

LocalBiz Connect 2.0 is a **unique full-stack web platform** designed for local businesses to showcase their profiles, engage with customers, collaborate with other businesses, and grow their presence online. Unlike traditional directories, this platform combines a **business directory, social timeline, interactive events, gamification, analytics, and identity verification** into one intuitive ecosystem.

The platform is fully **responsive, secure, and demo-ready**, built for deployment on **Lovable Cloud**.

Live Demo: https://local-biz-254.lovable.app
---

## **Key Features**

### 1. **Google Login & Secure Authentication**
- OAuth 2.0 login via Google for both customers and businesses.
- Role-based access control: Customer, Business Owner, Admin.
- Optional two-factor authentication for added security.

### 2. **Seller Identity Verification**
- Businesses submit government ID or business registration for verification.
- Admin approval required before activation.
- Verified businesses receive a “Verified Badge.”

### 3. **Business Status Feed (Social Timeline)**
- Post updates, promotions, events, and stories with images/videos.
- Feed supports likes, comments, and shares.
- Color-coded post types for clear visual distinction.

### 4. **Local Collaboration & Matchmaking**
- Businesses can post collaboration requests for cross-promotions or partnerships.
- Suggested matches recommended automatically based on category or service.

### 5. **Interactive Events & Workshops**
- Businesses create events with date, time, capacity, and optional price.
- Customers RSVP and receive confirmation notifications.
- Calendar and feed views for all events.

### 6. **Business Learning & Support Modules**
- Tutorials, guides, and tips on marketing, operations, and engagement.
- Progress tracking with achievement badges for completed modules.

### 7. **Gamification & Achievement Badges**
- Badges for completing profiles, posting regularly, responding to reviews, and hosting events.
- Optional leaderboard to encourage engagement and competition.

### 8. **Business Growth Insights Dashboard**
- Metrics: Profile views, post engagement, bookings, reviews, and story interactions.
- AI-driven suggestions for improving visibility and engagement.
- Visual charts, graphs, and quick action buttons.

### 9. **Micro-Communities & Topic Groups**
- Businesses join groups by industry or interest.
- Post updates, share knowledge, and discuss trends.
- Notifications for group activity.

### 10. **Local Customer Engagement Tools**
- Customers can post “Check-in Stories” at businesses.
- Businesses can create polls and surveys; results visible in dashboards.
- In-app notifications for engagement.

### 11. **Optional Advanced Feature: AR Local Discovery**
- Customers explore nearby businesses in augmented reality.
- Floating icons show posts, events, and promotions.
- Toggle between AR and standard map view.

### 12. **Security & Anti-Fraud Features**
- Admin monitoring for spam, fake reviews, and suspicious activity.
- Users can report fraudulent businesses or posts.
- Sensitive data encrypted in transit and at rest.

## **Installation & Deployment**

> This platform is built entirely on **Lovable Cloud**. Deployment and hosting are handled within Lovable. To get started:

1. Sign up or log in to **Lovable Cloud**.  
2. Import or create a new project and use the **LocalBiz Connect 2.0 prompt**.  
3. Configure **Google OAuth login** and identity verification settings.  
4. Pre-populate sample businesses, posts, events, and badges for demo.  
5. Test all features: status feed, booking/events, collaboration, polls, dashboards, and AR view.  
6. Deploy on Lovable Cloud for live demonstration.
